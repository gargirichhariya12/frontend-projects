Manage Your Salon in 4 Simple Steps

Running a salon has never been this easy. With our intuitive dashboard, you can manage your entire salon operations in just four simple steps—from adding your salon and services to handling bookings and analyzing performance. Save time, stay organized, and focus on what you do best—delivering exceptional customer experiences.

Add Your Salon

Easily register your salon by adding essential details like name, location, type, and a short description. Our streamlined process ensures you're up and running in just a couple of minutes—no technical skills needed.

Set Services & Staff

Define the services you offer—be it haircuts, facials, spa, or grooming—and assign each to the right staff member. Add pricing, duration, and more with a user-friendly interface that keeps everything perfectly organized.

Manage Bookings

Take full control of appointments with a dynamic calendar view. Schedule, reschedule, or cancel bookings in just a click. Stay updated with daily activities and reduce no-shows with automated reminders and updates.

Track & Analyze

Get powerful insights into your business performance. Track client visits, revenue, service popularity, and growth trends—all in one dashboard. Make informed decisions with real-time analytics and boost your efficiency.
